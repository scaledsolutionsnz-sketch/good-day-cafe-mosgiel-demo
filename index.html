All-Day Brunch

Fresh Baking

Croffles & Sweet Treats

Our story

A good day starts with a good coffee.

Good Day Cafe is a friendly, family-run spot in the middle of Mosgiel — the kind of place where the regulars get a wave, the coffee's poured just how you like it, and there's always room to sit a while.

We keep it simple: honest food, made fresh, and a welcome that makes locals and passing travellers feel equally at home. Whether it's a quick flat white before work or a slow weekend brunch with the family, we're glad to have you in.

  • Locally owned and run in Mosgiel
  • Fresh baking & brunch made in our kitchen
  • Dine in, takeaway or grab from the cabinet
