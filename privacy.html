Privacy Policy

How we handle any information you share with us. This is placeholder content for the demo.

Good Day Cafe respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle personal information collected through this website. It is provided as placeholder content for a website demonstration and should be reviewed before going live.

1. What we collect

If you send feedback through our private feedback form, we collect the details you provide — your name, email address, star rating and message. We do not use contact forms elsewhere on this site; enquiries are made by phone or email at your choice.

2. How we use it

We use your information only to respond to your feedback and to improve the food, coffee and service we offer at Good Day Cafe. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

3. How feedback is sent

Our feedback form uses a third-party form service (FormSubmit) to deliver messages to us by email. Your details pass through that service only to reach us and are handled according to their processing terms.

4. Cookies & analytics

This website may use basic cookies or analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This helps us improve the experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Third-party links

Our review page may direct you to Google to leave a public review. Once you leave our site, the privacy policy of that third party applies.

6. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 (NZ) you may ask to see or correct any personal information we hold about you. Get in touch and we'll help.
